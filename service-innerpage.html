Career Building Program

Get Your Expert Solution for Professional IT Career in Software industry.

Career Building Program is designed for graduates looking for a job. Candidates receive expert training and adequate exposure to industry – trends so that they transition into real-time with ease. Students are provided with the opportunity to work with our experienced technical staff and gain hands-on experience on our real-time projects.

We build an environment where the students can learn technical aspects to excel in a real-time environment. We boost the student’s confidence through resume building and provide documented placement assistance. Our system is very effective. We provide a relevant environment like gathering requirements, estimation points, design, implementation, deployments, pre-production and post-production activities.

Advanced Technical Program – with Placement Assistance (Duration 90 Days).

Swhizz provides placement assistance for the following career opportunities:

  • JAVA Application Development
  • Salesforce Application Development
  • DevOps Engineer
  • UI Development
  • Test Engineer Full Stack Development

Who can apply?

Candidate must be a Bachelor’s or Master’s graduate. Apply if you are:

  • A fresher interested in a career in the IT sector
  • An IT professional looking for a change in domain
  • An IT professional or a fresher with a career gap
  • A non-IT professional interested in a career in the IT sector
